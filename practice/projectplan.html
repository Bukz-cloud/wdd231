Site Name: ABY Elementary School

The site name is ABY Elementary School.This site name was selected as an online domain of an elementary school in line with the name of the school.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show potential clients the services we offer and to give them access to enrol their children without physically coming down to our school.

Scenarios

The following are the possible questions from our target audience:

  1. Is this school safe and well-organized?
  2. What are the school rules and values?
  3. How do I contact the school quickly
  4. Who teaches my child's class?
  5. What class is my child in?
  6. How do I register my child?
  7. What information do I need to provide?
